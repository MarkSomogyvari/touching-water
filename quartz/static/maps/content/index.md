# Touching Water: a Science Podcast


Welcome to the Touching Water Podcast portal! Here you can find all our episodes, shownotes and extra information about our podcast.

---
In ‘touching water’ an anthropologist and data scientist explore water with experts who work intimately with a well-known element. From hydrologists and engineers to local water experts and urban planners, our guests reveal how different people can approach, understand, and communicate about water.  
What emerges is not just knowledge about H2O, but new ways of seeing our relationship with water and people. Because how we touch water shapes how we understand our world.  
Hosts: Desirée Hetzel and Márk Somogyvári  

Supported by the recording studio at CMS at HU Berlin and the Geo.X network
