# Introducing Touching Water

We started this podcast because we are interested in exploring how to approach water.

We are Desirée and Márk, two water researchers. Márk, as a trained data scientist, is fascinated by water's dynamic presence—from subsurface flows to atmospheric moisture, exploring interfaces where rain becomes groundwater. Desirée, as a trained anthropologist, tries to understand hydrosocial relations, how different people work with water, and what happens when we see water as more than a resource.

We talk to each other about how we can approach water and with our guests explore how different scientific and everyday experts work with water—through measuring, modeling, fieldwork, or laboratory analysis. We learn about current challenges and research interests, understanding why it's important to think with water across disciplines and with the wider public.
